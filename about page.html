From Braamfontein with Love

We are a group of student friends and local artists living in the heart of Braamfontein. What started as late-night drawing sessions and sharing manga collections in our Juta Street apartment grew into Ink & Panel.

Our passion lies in the intersection of sequential art and community. We believe every comic deserves to be read, and every artist deserves a panel to call their own. Visit us to see how we are bringing the "Bleed" of local creativity to the "Structure" of the comic industry.
